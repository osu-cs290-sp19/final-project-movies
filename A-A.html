Premise of Hobbs & Shaw: This movie takes place two years after the events of the latest Fast and Furious movies (The Fate of the Furious). Luke Hobbs and Deckard Shaw are two men from different agencies, who dislike each other quite a bit, have to team up to take down a man named Brixton Lore (played by Idris Elba). Lore is a cyber-genetically enhanced international terrorist who has created a deadly virus that could threaten the human race... Just the usually buddy cop, save the world movie.
